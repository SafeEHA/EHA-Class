Introduction

The struggles we go through as young adults in Nigeria is sickening to even think about, the disadvantages we're at and how we suffer striving to survive.

Ontop of all that there's the pressure to not bring shame/disgrace to your family. Whatever you do, that has to be on the forefront of your mind.

Its not easy for anyone (young, old, disabled, poor, average even the rich) in Nigeria if i am being honest, that was why i created this blog.

I know alot of us aren't exactly willing to share our problems but i'm going to be putting my stories here just incase it helps a random reader,

Just so he/she knows theres someone out there that genuinely understands and can maybe work with to provide a lasting solution.

My journey Into Tech

I have the smartest tech person i know as a friend and he inspired me to say the least, he sent the link to this training infact.

The eHA Academy Female Only Cohort has been like a dream come true for me. i remember how much i panicked on the first zoom call,

i didn't stay the full 3 hours so i was freaking out on how i'll catch up. Amazingly everything has been running smoothly since then.

My mentor especially has been very helpful, the few other students i've interacted with have also been so amazing, and so far i've learnt so much.

The most intriguing part about the courses is building so many texts and colors from a blank page. My proudest moment is always when i find a "bug"

Or hit save and see wonders on my webpage. I am once again scared of the next Module but i have faith.
