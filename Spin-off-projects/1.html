This is Santorini Island, one of the most beautiful city in Greece. It has beautiful structures, lovely terraces, amazing sunsets and the most romantic spots for couples. The white color reflects the biggest part of the dazzling light, preventing the houses from getting warm and that was a basic goal of the traditional architecture. Making the houses heat resistant, the summers are much more tolerable and pleasant. If you're ever looking for the perfect place for a vacation then santorini should be your first option.

They say a place is never complete without food, Santorini is no exception, you will find the most exquisite 5star, 4star, 3star restaurants that would leave you very satisfied.
